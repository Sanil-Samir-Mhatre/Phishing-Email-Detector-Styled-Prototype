OWASP Phishing Guidelines

For Users: Recognizing and Handling Phishing Emails

  • Always verify the sender's email address.
  • Do not click on links from untrusted sources.
  • Hover over links to check their actual destination before clicking.
  • Avoid opening suspicious attachments.
  • Report phishing emails to your IT department or email provider.

For App Creators: Building Secure Apps to Detect Phishing

  • Utilize email header analysis to detect spoofing attempts.
  • Implement user training programs on phishing awareness.
  • Use machine learning models to detect phishing patterns.
  • Enforce two-factor authentication to add an extra layer of security.
  • Regularly update phishing detection algorithms with threat intelligence data.
